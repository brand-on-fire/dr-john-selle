Our Providers & Staff

I am pleased to introduce Nurse Practitioner, Victoria Chaudhary as a new addition to my growing practice!

Victoria received her Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) degree from USF and is nationally board certified as a Family Nurse Practitioner. She has been working in the healthcare field for over 9 years providing care to patients and their families.

Victoria is compassionate, empathetic and an excellent listener who combines the best of medicine and nursing to formulate effective, patient-centered, and holistic treatments. Working closely with Dr. Selle, she provides care to new and existing patients — addressing needs such as urgent and non-urgent follow-up appointments, preventative exams, and follow-up communication such as call backs and patient portal messages. She also performs women’s health annual exams, Pap smears, etc.

Outside the office, Victoria enjoys spending time with her husband and two small children. She likes to go biking, hiking, and traveling the world.

 What is a nurse practitioner?

Nurse Practitioners (NPs) are healthcare providers that deliver a unique blend of nursing and medical care. As primary care providers they assist patients in making better lifestyle and healthcare decisions. NPs can either have a master’s degree or doctorate degree (DNP).  NPs can prescribe medications, order labs and x-rays, diagnose and treat acute and chronic conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure, infections and injuries, and manage patient care. For more information go to: http://www.aanp.org/all-about-nps/what-is-an-np
